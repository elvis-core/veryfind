The bounty clock

One price. Rising every hour. First good report takes it.

VeryFind runs a single live bounty for the next accepted bug. It starts at 1 dollar and climbs by 1 dollar for every hour the crowd stays quiet, up to a 30 dollar ceiling. The instant anyone files, it resets to 1 dollar and starts climbing again.

So the math is simple. When reports are flowing, bugs are cheap and the client pays little. When the crowd goes quiet, the next bug becomes worth 10, 20, even 30 dollars, a standing invitation to come back and file. But it is a race: the first solid report claims the whole standing price, and everyone else is back to a dollar. Watch the meter, strike when it is high, do not wait too long or someone else grabs it.

You lock the price the moment you submit. You are paid once the operator validates the bug.

Payments

Paid in Canton Coin.

Every validated bug pays out in Canton Coin, the native asset of the Canton Network, straight to your Bron wallet by your brontag. No invoices, no bank details, no waiting on payroll.

Bron is our wallet of choice for payouts. Every tester gets a self-custody wallet with biometric, device-bound keys, so the coin you earn is yours alone, held on your own device.

Canton Coin (CC) settles on the Canton Network. Amounts shown in USD are converted to CC at payout.

  • On validation you earn the locked bounty. 70 percent is available immediately, 30 percent is held briefly as a quality hold, then released.
  • Payouts are batched per tester so Canton network fees stay negligible against your total, not eaten by tiny per-bug transfers.
  • Settlement is on-chain and yours to keep. You approve receipt in your own wallet.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is a brontag?

Your Bron wallet handle. It is how we pay you in Canton Coin.

Do I need to buy anything?

No. You test on the device you already own.

How fast do I get paid?

After an operator validates your bug. Payouts settle in Canton Coin to your Bron wallet.

Why does the price go up and down?

It is one live bounty for the next bug: up each idle hour to 30 dollars, back to 1 the moment someone files. First good report takes the standing price.

Can I register two devices?

One Bron account is one tester and one device. Re-registering updates your record, it does not create a second slot.

What if my bug is rejected?

No payout, and repeated low-quality reports can get you removed. Good reports build reputation.
